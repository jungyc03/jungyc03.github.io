project 1: the art of fighting

a typographic and visual exploration of violence as art.

this was the first project where i iterated a design in figma and then brought it to life through coding in html, css, and later on, jquery. thematically, i wanted something simple and elegant to reflect the nature of fighting — hence the name, the art of fighting. where some might view fighting as barbaric and crude, i see it as an art form. every move is calculated, every motion flows together without hitches. the intentionality behind each action is what makes it beautiful. there can be no wasted space, no wasted movements. that's why i chose black and white — with too many colors, there's too much going on, too much to focus on. in the middle of a fight, you can only focus on fighting and moving accordingly.

project 2: abroad

a website for a startup building a social media app designed to help Korean international students adjust to life abroad.

this project focused on translating the core features of a startup app into a clean, accessible web layout. the app is designed to support korean international students through community, resources, and connection — a digital space to feel a little less alone abroad. the soft gradient reflects the brand's color palette, drawing users in visually, while the direct, minimal text lets them know exactly what to expect. every word was chosen with care to foster a sense of belonging and community from the very first glance.
